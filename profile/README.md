## Welcome to the PRISM institute!

The *PRISM Institute* is a French institute for precision medicine in oncology. It is the fruit of a modern consortium **integrating medical and mathematical sciences**.

PRISM is an initiative of [Gustave Roussy](https://www.gustaveroussy.fr/en), [CentraleSupélec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr/en/) and [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en), in partnership with [Inserm](https://www.inserm.fr/en/home/). The [ARC Foundation for Cancer Research](https://www.fondation-arc.org/) and [UNICANCER](https://www.unicancer.fr/en/) are also members.
